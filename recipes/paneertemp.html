PANEER

Description

This is an Indian dish, it is basically the equivalent to cottage cheese in India. And yeah. It is yum!

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Spices - red chilli powder, salt, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala
  • Green chillies, if available
  • Yoghurt - to marinate
  • Ginger Garlic Paste, for added taste
  • 2-3 Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Marinate the paneer with spices + ginger garlic paste or yoghurt
  2. Caremalize onions, put green chillies, ginger garlic paste, fry tomatoes, add spices
  3. Can fry paneer first, if not - throw in the marinated paneer
  4. Fry and serve
